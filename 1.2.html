About St. Lawrence High School

St. Lawrence High School, Vashi, Navi Mumbai, is a co-educational school that was founded in 1996. It is affiliated with the Maharashtra State Board of Secondary and Higher Secondary Education and offers classes from Nursery to Class X.

The school's mission is to provide quality education to students and prepare them for a successful future.

Admissions

Admissions are open to students of all communities, irrespective of caste, creed, or religion. The school offers admissions to Nursery, Junior KG, Senior KG, and classes I to X.

Parents or guardians can apply for admission online through the school's website, or they can visit the school's admission office to obtain the application form. The admission process usually begins in the month of December.

Facilities

The school provides a range of facilities to its students, including:

  • Spacious classrooms with modern teaching aids
  • Well-equipped laboratories for science, mathematics, and computer science
  • A library with a large collection of books
  • An auditorium for cultural events and seminars
  • A playground for sports and outdoor activities
  • Transportation facilities for students
